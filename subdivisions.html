  • Private Studio Apartment

    Our private studio apartments are designed for international students who prefer privacy and independence. Each room includes a study area, a comfortable sleeping space, a kitchen, and a private bathroom. Located near train stations and universities, these apartments provide a peaceful environment for studying while reducing commuting time.

  • Shared Student Apartment

    Shared apartments are an affordable option for students who want to experience life with people from different cultures. Residents enjoy their own bedrooms while sharing common facilities such as the kitchen and living room. This choice helps students reduce expenses while building friendships and learning about different cultures.

  • University Area Housing

    These apartments are located close to major universities and public transportation. Students can easily attend classes, participate in campus activities, and travel to their part-time jobs without spending hours commuting. Living near campus provides greater convenience and helps students manage their busy schedules more effectively.

  • Affordable International Student Housing

    We provide clean, safe, and budget-friendly apartments specially designed for international students starting their academic journey in Japan. Whether you plan to stay for one semester or complete your entire degree, our housing offers comfort, security, and a welcoming environment that makes student life in Tokyo easier.
