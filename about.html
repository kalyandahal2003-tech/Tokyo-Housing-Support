Who We Are

Tokyo Student Housing is dedicated to helping international students find safe, comfortable, and affordable apartments in Tokyo. We understand the challenges of studying in a foreign country and aim to provide housing solutions that support both academic success and everyday living.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make student housing simple and accessible. We believe every international student deserves a clean, secure, and convenient place to live while studying in Japan. By offering apartments near universities and public transportation, we help students save time and enjoy a better quality of life.

Why Choose Us

We focus on the needs of international students by providing practical housing options at reasonable prices. Whether you are arriving in Japan for the first time or continuing your studies, our goal is to help you find accommodation that fits your budget and lifestyle.

Our Vision

We hope to build a welcoming community where students from different countries feel comfortable, supported, and connected. A good home allows students to concentrate on their education, experience Japanese culture, and create unforgettable memories during their university years.

About This Website

This website was created as an informational platform to introduce student housing options available for international students in Tokyo. It presents different apartment types, useful student living information, and resources that help students choose suitable accommodation before and during their studies in Japan.
